LANGUAGE ACQUISITION THEORIES

[SOURCES]
“Learning is a process by which behavior is originated or change through practice or training.”

(A) Cronbach

(B) ** Kingsley and Garry

(C) Pavlov, Ivan

(D) C. E. Skinner

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -According to Kingsley and Garry, “Learning is a process by which behavior is originated or change through practice or training.” In the words of C.E.Skinner, “Learning is the process of progressive behavior adoption.”

Concept note-2: -The much prominent educational psychologist E.A. Gates described learning as a modification of an individual’s behavior through experience and training. Learning includes those factors or forces that bring relatively enduring or permanent changes in our behavior.

Concept note-3: -Definitions of Learning According to Gardner Murphy “The term covers every modification of behavior to meet environmental requirements”. Kingsley and Garry defines it “The process by which behavior is organized or changed through practice or training.”

Concept note-4: -Crow and Crow defined learning as the process of acquisition of knowledge, habits and attitudes. According to E.A, Peel, Learning can be described as a change in the individual which takes place as a result of the environmental change.
