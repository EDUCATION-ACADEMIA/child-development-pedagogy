AUTISM

[SOURCES]
Lauren is a student with ASD. During group activities or unstructured time, Lauren becomes antagonistic with her peers and teacher. What would be a good course of action to help Lauren with this behavior?

(A) Remove Lauren from the classroom during these times.

(B) ** Use social stories to help her learn ways to concentrate in noisy environments.

(C) Use mnemonic strategies to help her control her behavior.

(D) Discuss Lauren’s behavior with her and set up a plan for consequences when the behavior occurs.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Lauren is a student in your class who has autism. During group activities or unstructured time, Lauren becomes very antagonistic with her peers and teacher. What would be a good course of action to help Lauren with this behavior? Use social stories to help her learn ways to concentrate in noisy environments.

Concept note-2: -Which of the following BEST describes the interpersonal relationships of students with emotional behavior disorders? They often have poor quality relationships with both peers and adults.

Concept note-3: -Functional behavioral assessment is used to identify specific relationships between a student’s behaviors and the circumstances that trigger those behaviors, especially those that impede the student’s or others’ ability to learn.

Concept note-4: -Intelligence tests (often called IQ tests) most commonly used to diagnose a learning disability include the Wechsler Preschool and Primary Scale of Intelligence (WIPPSI), Wechsler Intelligence Scale for Children (WISC), and the Wechsler Adult Intelligence Scale (WAIS).

Concept note-5: -It is estimated that 30% of children with autism spectrum disorders (ASD) will not develop speech sufficient to meet their communication needs (Anderson et al., 2007; Wodka, Mathy, & Kalb, 2013).
