What does Gus like to eat for breakfast every morning?

(A) ** cheerios, an apple, and a banana

(B) an apple, oatmeal and toast

(C) something different from the day before

(D) bacon, cheese, and eggs

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -You want to make sure you are eating a nice healthy breakfast that includes minimal sugar. I would suggest fruits such as bananas and apples in the morning as a healthy choice.

Concept note-2: -BUT eating Cheerios for breakfast in the morning with almond milk, a banana and a glass of OJ is always a quick healthy start.

Concept note-3: -Nothing like a banana at breakfast to keep those mid-morning cravings at bay. “Slice it up and add it to cereal or oatmeal, ” advised Giovinazzo. “It will add natural sweetness, so you may not need additional sugar."

Concept note-4: -As per studies, you should eat an apple in the morning hours. This is because apples are rich in dietary fiber, pectin, which is found in its peel. Since most people have digestive issues due to improper sleep or late eating habits, apples right in the morning, after waking up is a good idea.
