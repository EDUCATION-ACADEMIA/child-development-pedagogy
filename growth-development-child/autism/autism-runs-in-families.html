Autism runs in families

(A) ** TRUE

(B) FALSE

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -If someone in your family has autism spectrum disorder (ASD), you may be more likely to have a child with ASD. ASD can look very different from person to person, so taking a careful family health history can be important for early diagnosis.

Concept note-2: -Due to its lower prevalence in females, autism was always thought to have a maternal inheritance component. However, research also suggests that the rarer variants associated with autism are mostly inherited from the father.
