Find a sentence in Past Progressive

(A) ** I was reading a new book all the evening yesterday

(B) She is writing a letter to her friend now

(C) I did not travel to Wales last year

(D) I was scared of spiders

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -When the phone rang, he was writing an e-mail. While he was writing an e-mail, the phone rang. I was preparing dinner while Melanie was working upstairs. While Melanie was working upstairs, I was preparing dinner.

Concept note-2: -Both names are used. “I was reading” is Past Continuous/Progressive, and “I will be reading” is Future Continuous/Progressive.

Concept note-3: -Hello Tanya! Past participle of Answer is Answered. Hope it helps.

Concept note-4: -The past progressive, sometimes called the past continuous, is formed with the verb BE conjugated in the simple past (was | were) followed by a present participle. This is the formula: subject + BE + (verb+ing).
