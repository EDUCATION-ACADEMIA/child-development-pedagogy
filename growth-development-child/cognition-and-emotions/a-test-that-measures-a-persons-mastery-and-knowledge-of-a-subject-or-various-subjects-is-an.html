A test that measures a person’s mastery and knowledge of a subject or various subjects is a(n)

(A) ** Achievement test

(B) Aptitude test

(C) Intelligence test

(D) Personality test

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Hence, we can conclude that “A test which is used to measure the degree of mastery of skills, fundamental concepts, process and general knowledge of a subject attained by the student” is known as an achievement test.

Concept note-2: -The term achievement tests refers to tests designed to measure the knowledge, skills, and abilities attained by a test taker in a field, in a subject area, or in a content domain in which the test taker has received training or instruction.

Concept note-3: -An achievement test is a test of developed skill or knowledge. The most common type of achievement test is a standardized test developed to measure skills and knowledge learned in a given grade level, usually through planned instruction, such as training or classroom instruction.

Concept note-4: -An Achievement test is an assessment of developed knowledge or skill. The most common type of achievement test is a standardized test, such as the SAT, required for college entry in the United States.

Concept note-5: -The (AAT) ™ (Academic Aptitude and Achievement Test) is India’s largest and most popular scholastic aptitude test which has been written by over 1, 50, 000 students from all over India and abroad. This test has been used effectively by the top schools of India since the past 12 years.
