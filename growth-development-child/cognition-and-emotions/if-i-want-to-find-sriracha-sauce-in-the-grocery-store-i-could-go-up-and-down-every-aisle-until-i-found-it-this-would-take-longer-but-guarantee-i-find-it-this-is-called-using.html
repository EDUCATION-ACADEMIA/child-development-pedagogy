If I want to find sriracha sauce in the grocery store, I could go up and down every aisle until I found it. This would take longer, but guarantee I find it. This is called using

(A) ** An algorithm

(B) A heuristic

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The scarcity of Sriracha sauce was caused by a chili pepper shortage that has lasted for nearly two years now.

Concept note-2: -There are several hot sauces that originate in different parts of Asia, or which are Asian cuisine-inspired that you are likely to find in the “Asian food” aisle or section of your grocery store. This is where you’ll find Sriracha and Huy Fong chili garlic sauce, as well as pre-made curries and chili oil.

Concept note-3: -No, Sriracha hasn’t been discontinued, but production is suspended.

Concept note-4: -The shortage of hot sauce is expected to last through the summer and into the fall. The company annouced they plan to replenish stocks after Labor Day in September 2022.
