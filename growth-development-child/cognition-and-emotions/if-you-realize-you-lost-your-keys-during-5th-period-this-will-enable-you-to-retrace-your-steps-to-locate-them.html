COGNITION AND EMOTIONS

[SOURCES]
If you realize you lost your keys during 5th period, this will enable you to retrace your steps to locate them.

(A) Effortful processing

(B) ** Automatic processing

(C) iconic memory

(D) semantic encoding

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Hand the key in to the police. However, this option might be worth considering in the unlikely event that a key has clear identifying marks on it. If it’s possible to see which/whose door the key will open, then handing it in is a good idea.

Concept note-2: -If you have a key fob, smart key, or transponder key, you can track them. You will need a car key tracking device or an app connected to your key. The device consists of two parts-a remote and a receiver. You use the receiver to send a signal and then you can track it with the radio frequency or Bluetooth.

Concept note-3: -Can a lost key fob be tracked? Despite their high-tech nature, there’s no way to track your key fobs out-of-the-box… yet. Luckily for you, there are plenty of third-party options you can use to keep tabs on your key fobs until then!
