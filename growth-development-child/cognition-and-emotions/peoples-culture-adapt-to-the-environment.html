People’s culture adapt to the environment.

(A) ** True

(B) False

(C) I don’t know

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The given statement is true. The adaptations of humans are changes that occur in genes by the influence of the environment and its cultural practice.

Concept note-2: -A cultural adaption is the knowledge or behavior that enables humans or groups to adjust, survive, and thrive in their environment. One way humans culturally adapt to their environment is through the use of tools.

Concept note-3: -For thousands of years, humans have modified the physical environment by clearing land for agriculture or damming streams to store and divert water. As we industrialized, we built factories and power plants.

Concept note-4: -We can do this through cultural adaptation – that’s, by changing our behavior, norms, and values as a society to better adapt to our environment. Cultural adaptation is driven by the process of cultural evolution – the spread and maintenance of ideas within populations.
