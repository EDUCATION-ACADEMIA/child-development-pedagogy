Your memory of which of the following is an example of implicit memory?

(A) What you talked to your friend about in the hall yesterday

(B) The need to spend time reviewing for the upcoming psychology test

(C) ** Which way to turn the car key to start the engine

(D) That George Washington was the first president

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -So the way to turn the key to start the engine. So another aspect of the implicit memory is procedural memories such as walking, talking but um bike riding once you get the hang of it and also car driving.

Concept note-2: -Some examples of implicit memory include knowing how to play the piano, ride a bike; tie your shoes and other motor skills. These skills involve procedural knowledge which involves “knowing how” to do things.

Concept note-3: -Some examples of implicit memory include: Singing a familiar song. Typing. Brushing teeth.

Concept note-4: -Long-term memory. Implicit memory is a type of long-term memory related to the impact that activities and experiences can have on your behavior. You might also hear it referred to as nondeclarative memory. You access your implicit memory unconsciously without even thinking about it.
