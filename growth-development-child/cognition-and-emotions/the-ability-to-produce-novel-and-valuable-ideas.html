the ability to produce novel and valuable ideas.

(A) Critical Thinking

(B) Fixed Mindset

(C) ** Creativity

(D) Heuristics

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Creativity is the ability to generate, create, or discover new ideas, solutions, and possibilities. Very creative people often have intense knowledge about something, work on it for years, look at novel solutions, seek out the advice and help of other experts, and take risks.

Concept note-2: -Creativity: The ability to produce novel and valuable ideas.

Concept note-3: -Creativity is the ability to transcend traditional ways of thinking or acting, and to develop new and original ideas, methods or objects.

Concept note-4: -Creativity [1] is the production of novel and useful ideas by an individual or small group of individuals that work together. These individually produced creative ideas form the basis of innovation.

Concept note-5: -One of the most widely adopted definitions of creativity in contemporary philosophical debates says that creativity is the ability to produce novel and valuable objects (Boden 2004: 1; Sternberg and Lubart 1999), to which an element of intentionality or agency is often added (Kieran 2014; Stokes 2008; Gaut 2012).
