Which of the following is an example of the Short term memory?

(A) You forget to charge your Chromebook every night

(B) You remember the first 3 digits of a friends phone number but forget the rest

(C) You have trouble storing new memories after a traumatic brain injury

(D) ** You make a list of items to buy at the store but forget it at home. You remember the last few items on the list but forget the rest.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Examples of short term memory include where you parked your car this morning, what you had for lunch yesterday, and remembering details from a book that you read a few days ago.

Concept note-2: -Short-term memory is also known as primary or active memory. Research estimates that short-term memories only last for about 30 seconds. 3 When you read a line in a book or a string of numbers that you have to recall, that’s your short-term memory at work.

Concept note-3: -Function of RAM Because of its volatility, RAM can’t store permanent data. RAM can be compared to a person’s short-term memory, and a hard disk drive to a person’s long-term memory. Short-term memory is focused on immediate work, but it can only keep a limited number of facts in view at any one time.

Concept note-4: -Short-term memory refers to the memory systems in the brain involved in remembering pieces of information for a short period of time, often up to 30 seconds . Short-term memory creates a kind of “visuospatial” sketch of information the brain has recently absorbed and will process into memories later on.
