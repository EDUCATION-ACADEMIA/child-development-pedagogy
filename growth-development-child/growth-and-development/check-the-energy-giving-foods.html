Check the energy-giving foods

(A) ** bread

(B) ** rice

(C) beef

(D) ** corn

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -But corn can also be considered to be a whole grain because when you eat it, you have the whole kernel. Corn also contains a good source of fiber (about 5 grams per cup) which stabilizes the blood sugar and sustains energy.

Concept note-2: -Corn Nutrition Calories: 90. Protein: 3 grams (g) Fat: 1 g. Carbohydrates: 19 g.

Concept note-3: -Corn is high in dietary fiber, which is necessary for maintaining a healthy lifestyle. Fiber is the bulk in plant-based foods that your body doesn’t digest. Although it’s indigestible, the fiber in corn offers many other advantages, like regulating bowel movements, managing blood sugar levels, and more.
