What is an aquatic animal?

(A) An animal that lives on land

(B) An animal that is aqua-colored

(C) ** An animal that lives in water

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Aquatic animals like fish and frogs need to live in water for their survival. Hence they live in or around water bodies such as lakes or rivers. Fishes, whales, dolphins and turtles are aquatic animals.

Concept note-2: -All aquatic animals include fish, lobsters, dolphins, jellyfish, sharks, sea turtles, starfish, crabs, octopus, whales, seahorses, squid, swordfish, shrimp, killer whales, manta rays, otters, and oysters.

Concept note-3: -Answer: Aquatic animals: The animals which live in water. Examples: Prawn, Octopus, and Fish.

Concept note-4: -Animals living in water are called aquatic animals. All aquatic animals are not found in the same type of water. Many kinds of fish, snails, snake, are found in fresh water i.e. streams, lakes etc. Animals like dolphins, sharks, curbs, seals starfish, jelly are found in salty water i.e. the sea and oceans.

Concept note-5: -Examples of aquatic animals include crabs, frogs, seals, octopuses, seahorses, starfish, and fish, while crocodiles are a few water reptiles. 2.
