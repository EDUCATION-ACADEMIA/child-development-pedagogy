EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Marcia’s four identity stages are diffusion (low exploration, low commitment), foreclosure (low exploration, high commitment), moratorium (high exploration, low commitment), and achievement (high exploration, high commitment).

Concept note-2: -All domains of child development-physical development, cognitive development, social and emotional development, and linguistic development (including bilingual or multilingual development), as well as approaches to learning-are important; each domain both supports and is supported by the others.

Concept note-3: -Human development is a lifelong process of physical, behavioral, cognitive, and emotional growth and change.

Concept note-4: -Human development encompasses the physical, cognitive, and psychosocial changes that occur throughout a lifetime. Human development refers to the physical, cognitive, and psychosocial development of humans throughout the lifespan.
