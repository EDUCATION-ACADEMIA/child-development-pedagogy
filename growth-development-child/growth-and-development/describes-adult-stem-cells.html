Describes adult stem cells

(A) totipotent

(B) pleuripotent

(C) ** multipotent

(D) differentiated / specialized

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Adult stem cells, also called tissue-specific stem cells or somatic stem cells, are multipotent cells, which are responsible for replacing damaged and dead cells in the body (tissue maintenance role) [18]. They are found in specific microenvironment called stem cell niche.

Concept note-2: -Multipotent stem cells have the ability to differentiate into all cell types within one particular lineage. There are plentiful advantages and usages for multipotent stem cells. Multipotent Stem cells act as a significant key in procedure of development, tissue repair, and protection.

Concept note-3: -These stem cells are found in small numbers in most adult tissues, such as bone marrow or fat. Compared with embryonic stem cells, adult stem cells have a more limited ability to give rise to various cells of the body.
