A sunflower and tulip are planted together in the garden. The sunflower plant grows taller. Why?

(A) The sunflower received more water than the tulip plant.

(B) The sunflower received more fertilizer than the tulip plant.

(C) The sunflower received more sunlight than the tulip plant.

(D) ** Sunflower genes carry traits for taller stems than the tulip genes.
