Agribusiness is ____

(A) business related to the production, processing, and distribution of petroleum products

(B) ** business related to the production, processing, and distribution of agricultural products

(C) business related to the production, processing, and distribution of petrochemical industries products

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -What Is Agribusiness? Agribusiness is the business sector encompassing farming and farming-related commercial activities. It involves all the steps required to send an agricultural good to market, namely production, processing, and distribution.

Concept note-2: -The term agribusiness is used to refer to economic activities derived from or connected to farm products. In other words, crop production, as well as crop processing, transportation and distribution.

Concept note-3: -Agribusiness is made up of three components mainly: the agricultural input sector, the production sector and the processing-manufacturing sector.

Concept note-4: -Processing is defined as any activity that maintains or raises the quality or alters the physical or chemical characteristics of a material or object, or adds value to it in any way whatsoever. Processing can be as simple and quick as washing vegetables or it can be as involved and complicated as making cheese or wine.

Concept note-5: -Answer: Commercial farming, also known as agribusiness, is a farming method that involves growing crops, raising livestock, selling produce on the market, and making money.
