According to the Human Development Index, the Philippines has a ____ HDI.

(A) low

(B) very high

(C) ** high

(D) medium

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Philippines had a Human Development Index (HDI) score of 0.699, the UNDP said in its Human Development Report 2021 a year earlier the Philippine score was 0.718. The pandemic lowered our HD. But our country stabilized the situation and will level up our score for the following years.

Concept note-2: -The Philippines scored “high” in the United Nations Development Program’s (UNDP) Human Development Index, a measure of a country’s average achievements in three basic aspects of human development: health, knowledge and standard of living..

Concept note-3: -Switzerland had the highest level of the Human Development Index (HDI) worldwide in 2021 with a value of 0.962. With a score of 0.961, Norway followed closely behind Switzerland and had the second highest level of human development in that year.

Concept note-4: -The geometric mean of the three indices-that is, the cube root of the product of the indices-is the human development index. A value above 0.800 is classified as very high, between 0.700 and 0.799 as high, 0.550 to 0.699 as medium, and below 0.550 as low.
