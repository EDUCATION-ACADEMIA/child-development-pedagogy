A set of fact or principles analyzed in relation to one another and used to explain phenomena (a fact or behavior that can be observed)?

(A) ** Theory

(B) Fact

(C) Prediction

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A scientific theory is a structured explanation to explain a group of facts or phenomena in the natural world that often incorporates a scientific hypothesis and scientific laws.

Concept note-2: -A theory is a coherent explanation or interpretation of one or more phenomena.

Concept note-3: -Scientists distinguish between phenomena, which are their systematic observations, and theories, which are their explanations or interpretations of phenomena.

Concept note-4: -Empiricism is a philosophical theory applicable in many disciplines, including science and software development, that human knowledge comes predominantly from experiences gathered through the five senses.
