Fill in the blanks:Deoxyribonucleic acid is a molecule the shape of a ____ made of alternating sugar and ____ sides and complementary ____ as the rungs of the ladder.

(A) ** twisted ladder, phosphate, nitrogen bases

(B) straight ladder, nitrogen, phosphate bases

(C) straight ladder, phosphate, nitrogen bases

(D) twisted ladder, nitrogen, phosphate bases

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Double Helix Double helix, as related to genomics, is a term used to describe the physical structure of DNA. A DNA molecule is made up of two linked strands that wind around each other to resemble a twisted ladder in a helix-like shape.

Concept note-2: -Concept 19: The DNA molecule is shaped like a twisted ladder. Deoxyribose and phosphate molecules form the uprights and nucleotide pair form the rungs of the DNA ladder.

Concept note-3: -The sides of the ladder are made of alternating sugar and phosphate molecules. The sugar is deoxyribose.

Concept note-4: -DNA is made of two linked strands that wind around each other to resemble a twisted ladder-a shape known as a double helix.
