Which is the last stage of the human life cycle?

(A) Adolescence

(B) ** Late Adulthood

(C) Early Adulthood

(D) Adulthood

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Old-old (75-84). Adults in this age period are likely to be living independently, but often experience physical impairments since chronic diseases increase after age 75.

Concept note-2: -The stages of adulthood examined here include: Early Adulthood (ages 22–34). Early Middle Age (ages 35–44), Late Middle Age (ages 45–64), and Late Adulthood (ages 65 and older).

Concept note-3: -Human development is a predictable process that moves through the stages of infancy, childhood, adolescence, and adulthood.

Concept note-4: -What are the six stages of the human life cycle? The six stages of human development include the foetus, infancy, toddler years, childhood, puberty, adolescence, adulthood, middle age and senior years.

Concept note-5: -Adulthood is divided into three stages: (1) early, (2) middle, and (3) late adulthood. Early adulthood starts at age 18 or 21 and continues until the mid-30s. During middle adulthood, people start to show signs of aging. Late adulthood is when most people retire from work and reflect on their life.
