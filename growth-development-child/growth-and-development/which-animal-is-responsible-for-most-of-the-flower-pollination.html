Which animal is responsible for most of the flower pollination?

(A) praying mantis

(B) house mouse

(C) ** bumblebee

(D) American robin

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Honey bees, the most important crop pollinator, pollinate over 100 different fruits and vegetables, while bumble bees, which vibrate as they pollinate, are more efficient pollinators for plants such as tomatoes (Berenbaum 2007).

Concept note-2: -About 75% of North American plant species require an insect-mostly bees-to move their pollen from one plant to another to effect pollination.

Concept note-3: -Major agricultural pollinators include: Wild honey bees. Native honey bees are the most commonly known pollinator.
