The process by which human organisms produce others of their kind.

(A) ** Reproduction

(B) Fertilization

(C) Ovulation

(D) Menstruation

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Reproduction is the process by which living organisms produce more organisms of their kind to continue their species on earth.

Concept note-2: -Reproduction is the production of offspring. There are two main forms: sexual and asexual reproduction. In sexual reproduction, an organism combines the genetic information from each of its parents and is genetically unique.

Concept note-3: -Reproduction in Animals Sexual reproduction involves the fusion of male and female gametes. This process is known as fertilization. Fertilization can be external or internal. External fertilization is the process in which the male sperm fertilizes the female egg outside the femaleâ€™s body.

Concept note-4: -Reproduction is defined as a biological process in which an organism gives rise to young ones (offspring) similar to itself. The offspring grow, mature and in turn produce new offspring. Thus, there is a cycle of birth, growth and death. Reproduction enables the continuity of the species, generation after generation.

Concept note-5: -The process through which organisms produce another organism like its self is called reproduction. In general there are two types of reproduction: Sexual reproduction. In this type of reproduction, sex gametes fuse resulting to fertilization after which a young one develops.
