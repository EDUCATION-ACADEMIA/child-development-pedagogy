Dinda and Amel follow the same religion, while Sinta has a different religion from them. The three children had to work on group assignments. The division of tasks for each child is done evenly. Dinda and Amel have never treated Sinta arbitrarily even though she adheres to a different religion. Dinda and Amel have practiced the Pancasila precepts ____

(A) ** One

(B) Two

(C) Three

(D) Four

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A religious community is a community (group of people) who practice the same religion.

Concept note-2: -These might include, for example, wearing particular head coverings or other religious dress (such as a Jewish yarmulke or a Muslim headscarf), or wearing certain hairstyles or facial hair (such as Rastafarian dreadlocks or Sikh uncut hair and beard).

Concept note-3: -Religious diversity will enable you to be confident to attract and retain a workforce that reflects the society you operate in. It will give you the opportunity to address religious bias in the workplace and will bring diversity of thought, alongside many other benefits that are explored in more detail below.
