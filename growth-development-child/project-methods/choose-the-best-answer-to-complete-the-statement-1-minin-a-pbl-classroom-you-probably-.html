Choose the best answer to complete the statement. (1 min)In a PBL classroom, you probably ____

(A) won’t see multiple-choice quizzes.

(B) won’t see homework.

(C) won’t see teachers lecturing.

(D) ** might see any of these options:multiple choice quizzes, homework, or teachers lecturing.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -What is the definition of PBL? Refers to the general assumptions about what language is and about how learning a language occurs. Teaching method in which students learn by actively engaging in real-world and personally meaningful projects.

Concept note-2: -Step 1: Explore the issue. Gather necessary information; learn new concepts, principles, and skills about the proposed topic.

Concept note-3: -Project Based Learning (PBL) is “a teaching method in which students gain knowledge and skills by working for an extended period of time to investigate and respond to an authentic, engaging, and complex question, problem, or challenge” (Buck Institute for Education, n.d.).

Concept note-4: -Essential questions (also known as “driving questions") create authentic learning experiences to engage and motivate students. With strong essential questions, you’ll never hear students ask “why are we doing this?” The most engaging questions are open-ended and require research.
