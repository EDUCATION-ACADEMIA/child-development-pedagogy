What is the meaning of an organization?

(A) a body of persons eating together

(B) ** a body of persons acting together for some purpose

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -An organization is a group of people who work together, like a neighborhood association, a charity, a union, or a corporation. You can use the word organization to refer to group or business, or to the act of forming or establishing something.

Concept note-2: -Also, Keller (2015) defined organizational purpose as “an aspirational reason for being which inspires and provides a call to action for an organization and its partners and stakeholders and provides benefit to local and global society” (p. 1).

Concept note-3: -An association is an organisation of people with a common purpose and having a formal structure, like school clubs or religious groups which encourage and facilitate group work.

Concept note-4: -Let’s start with the meaning the word “organization.” Type the word into google, and you’ll find the definition: [noun] an organized body of people with a particular purpose, especially a business, society, association, etc.

Concept note-5: -An organized personality is a person who is naturally neat, punctual and detailed. Their habits and behaviors in life and at work are ordered, planned and efficient. They have natural organizational skills that other personality types might have to work to develop.
