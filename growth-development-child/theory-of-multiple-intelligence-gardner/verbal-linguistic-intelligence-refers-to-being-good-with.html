Verbal-linguistic intelligence refers to being good with

(A) music and dancing

(B) math and numbers

(C) animals and nature

(D) ** words, speaking, and books

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Verbal-linguistic intelligence enables people to communicate through language. It is related to the capacity and ability to handle the mother tongue or other languages, either orally or in writing, in order to communicate and express one’s thoughts.

Concept note-2: -Linguistic Intelligence Examples A person who loves to debate is an example of someone with linguistic intelligence. This person is likely to have a strong love of language, would likely exhibit a larger vocabulary, and understand meanings of words and phrases.

Concept note-3: -Linguistic intelligence, one of Howard Gardner’s eight multiple intelligences, involves the ability to understand and use spoken and written language.

Concept note-4: -Linguistic intelligence-also known as verbal-linguistic-is one of the many types of intelligence described in multiple intelligence theory. People with significant linguistic intelligence are often good at languages and enjoy reading and writing.
