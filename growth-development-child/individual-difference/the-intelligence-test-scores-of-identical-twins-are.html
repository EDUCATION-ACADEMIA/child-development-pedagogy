The intelligence test scores of identical twins are

(A) lower when they are raised together tan when they are raised apart

(B) ** greater than that for fraternal twins raised together

(C) the same whether they are reared together or apart.

(D) lower than that for any other blood relatives.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correlation of intelligence test scores for identical twins raised together is about +. 85. For fraternal twins raised together, it is about +. 60.

Concept note-2: -Intelligence scores are affected by both genetics and the environment. Siblings can differ in their genetic similarity, from 100% in identical twins to 50% in fraternal twins and different-aged siblings. They also differ in the similarity of their environment (more similar if raised together than apart).

Concept note-3: -No, identical twins do not have the same IQ, but their IQ is more similar than nonidentical twins and brothers and sisters in general. Parents and children also have a genetic intellectual relationship, but like identical twins, it is not a one to one correlation.
