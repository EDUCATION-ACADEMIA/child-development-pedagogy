____ is the different reactions of students when presented with new information. As their learning can be affected with their psyche towards a specific topic

(A) Values

(B) Psychomotor Skills

(C) ** Attitude

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The basic premise underlying any teaching is that the pupil has the capacity to learn and change and wants these things to happen regardless of past performance. Negative attitudes discourage, limit, and even prevent learning, positive change, and growth.

Concept note-2: -The presence or absence of specific attitudes, motivations and dispositions in a learner will: enhance or impede their capacity to learn. determine their willingness to grapple and persevere with, and make sense of discipline-based knowledge and content.

Concept note-3: -Existing research indicates that the attitude of a teacher is of great importance for students’ motivation to learn (Ross-Hill, 2009). The attitude of a positive and encouraging teacher impacts positively on the student’s attitude and behavior (Lee, 2019).

Concept note-4: -Attitudes towards learning are important factors on the learners’ levels of goal setting, problem solving abilities, their beliefs towards learning, their inner and external motivations in the process of learning and all the academic performances they perform.
