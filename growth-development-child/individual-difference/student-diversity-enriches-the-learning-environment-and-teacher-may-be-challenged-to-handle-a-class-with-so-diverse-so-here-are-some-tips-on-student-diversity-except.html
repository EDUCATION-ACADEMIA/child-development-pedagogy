INDIVIDUAL DIFFERENCE

[SOURCES]
Student diversity enriches the learning environment and teacher may be challenged to handle a class with so diverse. So, here are some tips on student diversity EXCEPT;

(A) ** Encourage learners to share other people history and experiences.

(B) Integrate learning experience and activities which promote students multicultural and cross-cultural awareness.

(C) Use varied instructional methods to accommodate student diversity in learning styles.

(D) Diversity your methods of assessing and evaluating student learning.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Students who learn about different cultures during their education feel more comfortable and safe with these differences later in life. This allows them to interact in a wider range of social groups and feel more confident in themselves as well as in their interactions with others.

Concept note-2: -Student diversity can promote harmony. Through student-centered teaching strategies, diverse students can be encouraged to interact and collaborate with one another on learning tasks that emphasize unity of effort while capitalizing on their diversity of backgrounds.
