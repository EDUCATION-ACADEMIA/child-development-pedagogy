Workers are organised into teams or groups.

(A) Physical needs

(B) Safety needs

(C) ** Social needs

(D) Esteem needs

(E) Self-actualisation

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Share diverse perspectives. Pool knowledge and skills. Hold one another (and be held) accountable. Receive social support and encouragement to take risks.

Concept note-2: -Work Groups and Work Teams A work team has members who work interdependently on a specific, common goal to produce an end result for their business. A work group is two or more individuals who are interdependent in their accomplishments and may or may not work in the same department.

Concept note-3: -A team is a group of people who do collective work and are mutually committed to a common team purpose and challenging goals related to that purpose. Collective work and mutual commitment are the key characteristics.

Concept note-4: -When people share an elevator ride, they are a group; when the elevator gets stuck, they become a team. Individuals that get on the same elevator most likely share some things, like: they work in the same building and on the same floor or they may work for the same boss and have similar interests.
