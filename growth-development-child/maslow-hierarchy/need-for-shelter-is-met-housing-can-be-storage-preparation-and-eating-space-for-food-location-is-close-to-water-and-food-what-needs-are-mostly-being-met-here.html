MASLOW HIERARCHY

[SOURCES]
Need for shelter is met. Housing can be storage, preparation, and eating space for food. Location is close to water and food. What needs are mostly being met here?

(A) ** Physiological

(B) Safety

(C) Love & Belonging

(D) Self-Actualization

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Shelter is a basic human need crucial for survival in cases of natural hazards or conflict. It provides security, personal safety and protection from the weather, and prevents ill health and disease. Adequate housing provides people with dignity and the opportunity to lead a normal life.

Concept note-2: -We must have food, water, air, and shelter to survive. If any one of these basic needs is not met, then humans cannot survive. Before past explorers set off to find new lands and conquer new worlds, they had to make sure that their basic needs were met.

Concept note-3: -Cephalic responses: food stimulates physiological responses that are preparatory to eating, digesting, metabolizing, and storing the food, the smell and taste of food elicit the secretion of saliva (mouth), gastric juices (stomach), and insulin (from pancreas).

Concept note-4: -Food, water, clothing, sleep, and shelter are the bare necessities for anyone’s survival. For many people, these basic needs can not be met without the aid of charitable organizations. A reliable place to receive a meal can be what’s needed for a person to focus on obtaining higher needs.
