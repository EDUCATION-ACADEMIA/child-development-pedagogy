PIAGET

[SOURCES]
Four-year-old Jennifer mistakenly believes that her mother would like to receive a toy doll as a Christmas present because she herself would like to. This best illustrates Piaget’s concept of:

(A) accommodation

(B) attachment

(C) object permanence

(D) ** egocentricism

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Solution: According to Piaget’s Genetic Epistemology, Jennifer is in the Pre-Operational Stage of Cognitive development because she reflected the state of egocentric behaviour which is the characteristic of pre-operational stage.

Concept note-2: -Four-year-old Jennifer mistakenly believes that her mother would like to receive a toy doll as a Christmas present.

Concept note-3: -Summed up, some researchers have described it this way: Authoritative parents are highly demanding (like authoritarian parents), but they are also very responsive to their children’s needs (Maccoby and Martin 1983).

Concept note-4: -When Tommy’s mother hides his favorite toy under a blanket, he acts as though it no longer exists and makes no attempt to retrieve it.

Concept note-5: -If children cannot grasp the principle of conservation, they are unable to: recognize that the quantity of a substance remains the same despite changes in its shape.
