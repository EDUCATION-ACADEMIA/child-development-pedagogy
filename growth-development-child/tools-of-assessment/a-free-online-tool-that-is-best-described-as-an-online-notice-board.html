A free online tool that is best described as an online notice board.

(A) jamboard

(B) ** padlet

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Padlet is a free online tool that is best described as an online notice board. Padlet can be used by students and teachers to post notes on a common page. The notes posted by teachers and students can contain links, videos, images and document files.

Concept note-2: -What is Padlet? Padlet is an online post it wall. It allows individuals (and large/ small groups) to post their comments, questions, and resources in one place that is easily accessible to everyone.

Concept note-3: -Padlet (previously known as Wallwisher) is a free Web 2.0 application that provides a virtual wall and collaborative space accessible from any Internet-enabled device.

Concept note-4: -Is Padlet free? Padlet’s free version for all users allows you to create up to three Padlet boards at a time. You can erase and create new ones to stay under the limit.

Concept note-5: -Padlet is a great place for gathering ideas, sharing them and modifying them later. It’s like a living, breathing webpage. Users can add links, YouTube videos, files and images to Padlet notes. They can move and arrange them.
