Which of the following tools allows students to create video responses to a given question?

(A) Edulastic​

(B) Spiral

(C) ** Flipgrid

(D) Socrative​

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Flipgrid is a social learning platform that allows educators to ask a question either through a video or voice recording, then the students can respond in a video or voice recording.

Concept note-2: -How Do I Upload a Pre-Recorded Video to FlipGrid? 1. Tap to record-Tap next to the record button; it says “Options” under the three dots. Choose “upload clip.” Select your video on your computer or device an finish the steps to submit to li ri .

Concept note-3: -View and comment on videos Scroll down on the topic page to view video responses. While viewing a video you can add a text comment or record a video comment. You will get an email notification when someone comments on your video. Videos and comments can be viewed by anyone with the link and a UH login.

Concept note-4: -Flipgrid is a website that allows teachers to create “grids” to facilitate video discussions. Each grid is like a message board where teachers can pose questions, called “topics, ” and their students can post video responses that appear in a tiled grid display.
