panfrying, deep frying, grilling, broiling, and roasting are all examples of the ____ method

(A) ** dry heat

(B) moist heat

(C) combination

(D) braising

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Common dry-heat cooking methods include: pan frying, searing, roasting, sauteing, sweating, stir-frying, shallow-and deep-frying, grilling, broiling, baking and rotisserie cooking.

Concept note-2: -Deep frying is considered a Dry Heat Method of cooking. It uses heat conduction and natural convection to transfer heat to food submerged in fat. Thus begins that golden brown delight known as deep fried food.

Concept note-3: -Examples of dry-heat methods include: Roasting and Baking. Grilling and Broiling. Saut√©eing and Pan-Frying.

Concept note-4: -Pan Broil is a cooking technique used for thin steaks, thin chops and fish fillets. It is a dry cooking method done in a frying pan on top of the stove with no added fat or liquid.
