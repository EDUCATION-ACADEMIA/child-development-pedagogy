A lighter ingredient gently incorporated into another, heavier ingredient by a rubber scraper.

(A) mix

(B) cream

(C) ** folding in

(D) combine

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Stirring: This is mixing the ingredients by hand using a rubber spatula, spoon, or whisk.

Concept note-2: -A technique used to gently combine a light, airy ingredient (such as beaten egg whites) with a heavier one (such as whipped cream or cake mix). The lighter mixture is poured on top of the heavier one in a large bowl.

Concept note-3: -Tips for Folding Ingredients Grab a flexible spatula: A wide, flexible silicone spatula works best for folding.
