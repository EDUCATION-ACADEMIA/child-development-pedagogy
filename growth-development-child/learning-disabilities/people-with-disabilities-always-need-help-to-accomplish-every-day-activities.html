[SOURCES]
People with disabilities always need help to accomplish every day activities.

(A) True

(B) ** False

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -People with disabilities always need help to accomplish everyday activities. False. People with disabilities may need help sometimes but it doesn’t mean they are always dependent on others.

Concept note-2: -To help nurture responsible individuals that contribute to society. Disabilities do not make a person a less functional member of the community. By giving equal opportunities and the corresponding respect, we grow a sense of confidence in the people with disabilities and cultivate a sense of belonging.

Concept note-3: -False – People with disabilities may need some special attention, but it should not be assumed in every case. Your customers want to be treated with dignity, so consider discreetly asking each one if help is needed.

Concept note-4: -People-first language is the best place to start when talking to a person with a disability. If you are unsure, ask the person how he or she would like to be described.

Concept note-5: -It’s safe to assume that people with disabilities usually need help. FALSE. Most people with disabilities prefer to be independent. When offering help to someone with a disability, always ask first, wait for their response and then ask them about the best way to provide the help they need.
