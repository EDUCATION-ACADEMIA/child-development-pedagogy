What is the prevalence of learning disabilities?

(A) Just under 1/2% of school age students

(B) Just under 1% of school age students

(C) ** Just under 5% of school age students

(D) Just under 10% of school age students

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Scholastic performance falls below the individual’s chronological age, and the impairment rolls out across the domains like reading, reading comprehension, written expression, spelling and mathematics. Prevalence of specific learning disability in India ranges from 5%–15% in various studies [2].

Concept note-2: -Learning Disabilities Prevalence Compared to all children in the US, they represent about 1.69% of the population.

Concept note-3: -In India, the prevalence of SLD is reported to vary from 3% to 10%. In India, although SLD is included as one of the disabilities according to the Rights of Persons with Disability Act of 2016, the screening and diagnosis of SLD are complicated.

Concept note-4: -The percentage of children aged 3–17 years diagnosed with a developmental disability increased–from 16.2% in 2009–2011 to 17.8% in 2015–2017.
