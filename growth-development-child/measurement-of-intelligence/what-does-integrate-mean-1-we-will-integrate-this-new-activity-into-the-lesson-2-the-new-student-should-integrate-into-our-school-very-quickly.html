What does integrate mean? 1. We will integrate this new activity into the lesson. 2. The new student should integrate into our school very quickly.

(A) separate, split, divide into parts

(B) collect, capture

(C) ** blend, meld, combine

(D) necessary, essential

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Integration refers to exceptional students being partially taught in a mainstream classroom. Activities are adapted so the student can “fit in” with their mainstream peers while learning skills that may be better practiced in a room with more age-appropriate peers.

Concept note-2: -transitive verb. : to form, coordinate, or blend into a functioning or unified whole : unite. : to incorporate into a larger unit.

Concept note-3: -What Are Integrated Lessons? In general, integration is defined as the process of combining two or more things into one. Within education, integrated lessons take on a similar meaning in that they combine two or more concepts into one lesson.

Concept note-4: -Integrated activities means jointly planned, funded, and interwoven activities between research and extension to solve problems. Integrated activities are those activities that include some combination of teaching, education, and research.
