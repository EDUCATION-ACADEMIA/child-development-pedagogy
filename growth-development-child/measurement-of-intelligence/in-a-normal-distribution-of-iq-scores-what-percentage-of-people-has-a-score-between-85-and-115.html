In a normal distribution of IQ scores, what percentage of people has a score between 85 and 115?

(A) 35 percent

(B) 54 percent

(C) ** 68.26 percent

(D) 79.32 percent

(E) 95.44 percent

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Based on the 68-95-99.7 Rule, approximately 68% of the individuals in the population have an IQ between 85 and 115.

Concept note-2: -The one-standard-deviation rule implies that about 32 percent of the population have IQ scores more than 15 points away from 100: 16 percent above 115 and 16 percent below 85.

Concept note-3: -68% of people have IQs between 85 and 115 (100 +/-15). 95% have IQs between 70 and 130 (100 +/-(2*15). 99.7% have IQs between 55 and 145 (100 +/-(3*15).

Concept note-4: -70 to 84: Borderline mental disability. 85 to 114: Average intelligence. 115 to 129: Above average or bright. 130 to 144: Moderately gifted.
