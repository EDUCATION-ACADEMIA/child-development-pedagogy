If you have a high “g” score, you’re probably going to do well:

(A) with reading others’ emotions

(B) ** in school

(C) with procedural tasks

(D) in athletics

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Good grades open the door to college and university High school grades matter most if you have hopes of going to college. The grade point average (GPA) is one factor that colleges may consider when deciding to accept or deny a student.

Concept note-2: -No. We have hundreds and thousands of examples of people who have done pretty well in their lives without having good grades in schools.

Concept note-3: -A-is the highest grade you can receive on an assignment, and it’s between 90% and 100% B-is still a pretty good grade! This is an above-average score, between 80% and 89% C-this is a grade that rests right in the middle.
