MEASUREMENT OF INTELLIGENCE

[SOURCES]
____ learners may tap a pencil or foot while studying.

(A) visual

(B) auditory

(C) ** kinesthetic

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Kinaesthetic learning happens when we have a hands-on experience. An example of a kinaesthetic learning experience is when a child learns to use a swing or to ride a bike. They can read instructions or listen to instructions, but deep learning occurs via the process of doing.

Concept note-2: -Kinesthetic or tactile learners need to physically touch or try something in order to learn the concept best. This style is often called multi-sensory learning because tactile learners hear or see to learn, and then complete their learning by trying it out themselves.
