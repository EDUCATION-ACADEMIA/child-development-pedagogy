(A) Diary

(B) ** Portfolio

(C) Anecdotal record

(D) Observation report

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A portfolio is a systematic collection of student work that represents student activities, accomplishments, and achievements over a specific period of time in one or more areas of the curriculum. There are two main types of portfolios: Showcase Portfolios: Students select and submit their best work.

Concept note-2: -Thus it is clear that project is the collection of tasks done by students in a given period.

Concept note-3: -A portfolio is a purposeful collection of student work samples, student self-assessments, and goal statements that reflect student progress. Portfolios are powerful tools that allow students to see their academic progress from grade to grade.
