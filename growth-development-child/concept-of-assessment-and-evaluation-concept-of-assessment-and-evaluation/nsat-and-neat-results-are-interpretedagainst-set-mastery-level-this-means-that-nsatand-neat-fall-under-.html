CONCEPT OF ASSESSMENT AND EVALUATION CONCEPT OF ASSESSMENT AND EVALUATION

[SOURCES]
NSAT and NEAT results are interpretedagainst set mastery level. This means that NSATand NEAT fall under ____

(A) Aptitude test

(B) ** Criterion-referenced test

(C) Intelligence test

(D) Norm-referenced test

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Well-known examples of criterion-referenced tests include Advanced Placement exams and the National Assessment of Educational Progress, which are both standardized tests administered to students throughout the United States.

Concept note-2: -Norm-Referenced Interpretation. Scores indicate how an individual compares. with the norming population (i.e. individuals’ scores are compared with the scores of. others)

Concept note-3: -Criterion-referenced tests are standardized tests that measure an individual’s performance against a set of predetermined criteria or performance standards (e.g., descriptions of what an individual is expected to know or be able to do at a specific stage of development or level of education).

Concept note-4: -Schools use criterion-referenced tests to assess the specific knowledge and skills students have most likely learned in order to assess how close a student is to mastering a specific standard.
