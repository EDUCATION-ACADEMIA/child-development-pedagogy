FREUDS PERSONALITY THEORY

The elements of a dream that people cannot usually recall and the symbolism that gives the real meaning of a dream is what type of content?

(A) ** Latent Content

(B) Manifest Content

(C) Dream Content

(D) Dreamwork

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -According to Freud, the latent content of a dream is the hidden psychological meaning of the dream. This content appears in disguise symbolically and contains things that are hidden from conscious awareness, often because it may be upsetting or traumatic.

Concept note-2: -Answer and Explanation: The four dream processes described by Sigmund Freud are condensation, displacement, secondary revision, and considerations of representation.

Concept note-3: -The manifest content of a dream is what literally happens in it, while latent content is what the dream symbolizes. Freud believed that the manifest content of a dream was a distorted version of the latent content, which was the dreamer’s true desire or fear.

Concept note-4: -A common man cannot know the real implication behind the latent content of the dream. According to Brown (1940) “By the latent content of the dream is meant the underlying unconscious wish which comes into consciousness through adopting the disguise of the manifest content”.

Concept note-5: -Freud believed that the unconscious (id) expresses itself in dreams as a way of resolving repressed or unwanted emotions, experiences, and aggressive impulses.
