What degree did Freud first go to school for?

(A) Elementary Education Degree

(B) Microbiology Degree

(C) Business Degree

(D) ** Medical Degree

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -After graduating (1873) from secondary school in Vienna, Sigmund Freud entered the medical school of the University of Vienna, concentrating on physiology and neurology; he obtained a medical degree in 1881.

Concept note-2: -In 1859–1860, the Freud family first moved from Freiberg to Leipzig, and then to Vienna, where Freud spent most of his life [1, 2]. In 1873, Freud graduated with distinction from gymnasium and in the same year he began studying medicine at the University of Vienna.

Concept note-3: -Sigmund Freud was born May 6, 1856, in Moravia. Freud performed very well in school as a child, and at age 17, went off to medical school at the University of Vienna.

Concept note-4: -Early in his career, Freud became greatly influenced by the work of his friend and Viennese colleague, Josef Breuer, who had discovered that when he encouraged a hysterical patient to talk uninhibitedly about the earliest occurrences of the symptoms, the symptoms sometimes gradually abated.

Concept note-5: -For these reasons, it was above all with the city of Vienna that Freud’s name was destined to be deeply associated for posterity, founding as he did what was to become known as the first Viennese school of psychoanalysis from which flowed psychoanalysis as a movement and all subsequent developments in this field.
