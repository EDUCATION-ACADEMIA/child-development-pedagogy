EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The chemical formula for water is H2O, which means each molecule of water consists of one oxygen atom chemically bonded to two hydrogen atoms. Thus, water is a compound.

Concept note-2: -Water is a compound because it is made up of water molecules. There is no such thing as water atoms. Water molecules are made of hydrogen and oxygen atoms, in the definite proportion of two hydrogens for one oxygen.

Concept note-3: -The chemical formula of water is H2O, as it has 2 hydrogen atoms and 1 oxygen atom. So, the chemical name of water is dihydrogen oxide.
