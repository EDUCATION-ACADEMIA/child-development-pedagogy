What is it called when a material goes from solid to gaseous state?

(A) evaporation

(B) ** sublimation

(C) condensation

(D) deposition

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Sublimation. The process of converting solid to gas directly is called sublimation. Ex: Dry ice is solid carbon dioxide. At room temperature it directly changes into gas from solid state.

Concept note-2: -Sublimation is a type of phase change that takes place when a solid turns directly into a gas, skipping the liquid phase.

Concept note-3: -Sublimation is the transition of a substance directly from the solid phase to the gas phase without passing through the intermediate liquid phase (Table 4.8, Fig. 4.2). Sublimation is an endothermic phase transition that occurs at temperatures and pressures below the triple point of a chemical in the phase diagram.

Concept note-4: -Sublimation is the process in which solid directly changes to gas.

Concept note-5: -desublimation (countable and uncountable, plural desublimations) (physics) deposition (transformation of gas into solid without an intermediate liquid phase)
