As depth increases ____

(A) Things become shallow

(B) ** temperature decreases, pressure increases, and light decreases

(C) It is more salty

(D) An Ocean Current

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -As you move deeper into the Earth, the pressure increases. Similarly, the deeper you move into the Earth, the temperature increases. Thus, with increasing depth, the pressure and temperature are greater.

Concept note-2: -Water gets colder with depth because cold, salty ocean water sinks to the bottom of the ocean basins below the less dense warmer water near the surface.

Concept note-3: -Temperature is directly proportional to the depth inside Earth. In simple terms, it means the temperature increases as the depth increases inside Earth. Moreover, as the depth increases inside Earth, the presence of hot objects (magma, lava, rocks) increase, which increases the temperature.

Concept note-4: -The pressure at a given point in the earth increases with the depth of that point.
