How often should a pre-assessment be given?

(A) Once at the beginning of the semester

(B) ** Once per unit

(C) Once per lesson

(D) Once per day

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Preview pre-assessments measure the knowledge, skills, and dispositions that make up the learning goals of a forthcoming unit or task. They identify for students what they will be learning and perhaps why it is important.

Concept note-2: -Answer: Assessment is not completed once every grading period, since the main aim of it, is to increase and improve the students learning.
