____ was announced as the winner of Malaysia’s Flag design competition.

(A) ** Mohamad Bin Hamzah

(B) Mahathir Bin Mohamad

(C) King George VI

(D) Hamzah Bin Mohamad

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Over 300 artists took part and on 15 November 1949, only three flags were chosen for the finals. The winning flag, which is the Jalur Gemilang we have today, is a result of Mohamed bin Hamzah’s hard work.

Concept note-2: -The Malayan flag was designed by Mohamed Hamzah, a 29-year-old architect working for the Public Works Department (JKR) in Johor Baharu, Johor. He entered the national flag design competition with two designs that he had completed in two weeks.

Concept note-3: -The flag of Malaysia is named ‘Jalur Gemilang’ which means ‘Stripes of Excellence’ or ‘Stripes of Glory’ in Malay. It was named in 1997 by the Prime Minister of that time, Tun Dr.

Concept note-4: -The Crescent in the flag represents Islam – The country’s state religion. The star consists of 14 points. Each point is to symbolize the unity between the entities. The color blue in their flag is to represent the unity of Malaysian people, and the yellow is the royal color of Malay Rulers.
