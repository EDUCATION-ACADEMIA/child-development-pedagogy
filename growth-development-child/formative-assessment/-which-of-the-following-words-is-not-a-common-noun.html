· Which of the following words is not a common noun?

(A) ** game stop

(B) videogame

(C) food

(D) bathroom

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The common noun is simply a noun that refers to a class of objects, as opposed to a specific object. For example, the word “restaurant” would be a common noun. However, the specific name “Olive Garden” would not be a common noun. The opposite of a common noun is a proper noun.

Concept note-2: -Answer and Explanation: The noun ‘game’ is a common noun that is not capitalized. It is not the name of a specific ‘game, ’ but just a ‘game’ in general. The name of a specific trademarked game, such as ‘Monopoly’ or ‘Trouble’ would be capitalized because they are proper nouns.

Concept note-3: -Street, closet, bathroom, school, mall, gas station, living room; all of these places are things, and thus they are common nouns.

Concept note-4: -The correct answer is ‘catchy’.
