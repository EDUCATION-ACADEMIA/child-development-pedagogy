What advice did Ron Forbes give to Evelyn?

(A) see how the fingers move on the instruments

(B) ** sense through different parts of the body

(C) reading the notes of the music

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Answer: While most of her teachers discouraged her percussionist Ron Forbes was the first person to notice Evelyn’s potential and helped her continue with her music. He began by tuning two large drums to different notes. He asked Evelyn not to listen through her ears but to try and sense the sound in some other manner.
