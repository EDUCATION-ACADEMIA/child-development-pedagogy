FORMATIVE ASSESSMENT

[SOURCES]
Examples of natural resources include ____

(A) popcorn, gum, and chocolate.

(B) ** water, plants, animals, and sun.

(C) books, games, and action figures.

(D) pens, markers, and crayons.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Common examples of natural resources include air, sunlight, water, soil, stone, plants, animals and fossil fuels.

Concept note-2: -Oil, coal, natural gas, metals, stone and sand are natural resources. Other natural resources are air, sunlight, soil and water. Animals, birds, fish and plants are natural resources as well.

Concept note-3: -Biotic resources are the living thing of the biosphere. Plants and animals have life. Therefore plants and animals are biotic resources.

Concept note-4: -Natural resources include oil, coal, natural gas, metals, stone, and sand. Air, sunlight, soil, and water are other natural resources. Thus, Natural resources are valuable as they are used to support life and meet the needs of people.

Concept note-5: -The Sun is a renewable abiotic energy resource that provides energy in the form of heat and light. The energy obtained from the Sun is called solar energy.
