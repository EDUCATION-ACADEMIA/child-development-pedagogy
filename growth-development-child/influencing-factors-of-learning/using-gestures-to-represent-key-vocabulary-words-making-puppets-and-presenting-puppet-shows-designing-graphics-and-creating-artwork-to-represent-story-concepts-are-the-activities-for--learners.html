INFLUENCING FACTORS OF LEARNING

[SOURCES]
Using gestures to represent key vocabulary words. Making puppets and presenting puppet shows. Designing graphics and creating artwork to represent story concepts are the activities for ____ learners.

(A) ** kinesthetic

(B) visual

(C) reflective

(D) auditory

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Using gestures to represent key vocabulary words. Making puppets and presenting puppet shows. Designing graphics and creating artwork to represent story concepts.

Concept note-2: -Definition: A kinesthetic-tactile learning style requires that you manipulate or touch material to learn. Kinesthetic-tactile techniques are used in combination with visual and/or auditory study techniques, producing multi-sensory learning.

Concept note-3: -Kinesthetic Communicators Kinesthetic learners notice and remember physical sensations. If they walk out of a stressful job interview, they may not remember what questions they were asked or what the interviewer was wearing, but they will remember how they felt.

Concept note-4: -Kinesthetic Products: diramas, puzzles, games, exhibits. Written Products: journals, diaries, logs, reports, essays, position papers. Portfolio Assessment: students collect work samples over time to demonstrate how much they have learned and the process they used to do it.
