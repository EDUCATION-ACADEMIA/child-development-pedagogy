“You go, I stay” is an ____ style, it uses pronouns with intent to convey social interactive meaning.

(A) Conservative

(B) ** Expressive

(C) Referential

(D) Regressive

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -What are pronouns, and why are they important? Typically, when we communicate in English, we use pronouns to refer to ourselves and one another, such as “I, ” “you, ” “he, ” or “she.” To create and maintain an inclusive environment, it’s important that we don’t make assumptions about a person’s pronouns.

Concept note-2: -A pronoun is a word that replaces a noun or noun phrase. Using pronouns correctly eliminates unnecessary noun repetition in your writing. Unnecessary repetition: Mary is in Mary’s office, but Mary asked not to be interrupted. Pronoun use eliminates repetition: Mary is in her office, but she asked not to be interrupted.

Concept note-3: -The main function of pronouns is to replace nouns. Because of this, they are used in sentences in similar ways to nouns. Like nouns, pronouns commonly serve as the subject of a sentence, followed by a verb (a word expressing an action).

Concept note-4: -Pronouns are words that are used to take the place of nouns in sentences. They usually refer back to a noun used earlier in the sentence, and they must match the number, point of view, and gender of the noun. We use pronouns to make sentences clearer, less awkward, and smoother.
