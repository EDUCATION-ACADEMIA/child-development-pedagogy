Another example of adolescent egocentrism is the ____ having an inflated opinion of one’s importance, feeling special and unique.

(A) Stereotype threat

(B) ** Personal fable

(C) Social constructivism

(D) Self-serving bias

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Adolescent Egocentrism Examples Spending an inordinate amount of time grooming for fear of being ridiculed for not looking just right. Refusing to go to school because they have a pimple or other flaw and believe that all their classmates will be staring at them.

Concept note-2: -The Personal Fable is a belief held by many adolescents telling them that they are special and unique, so much so that none of life’s difficulties or problems will affect them regardless of their behavior.

Concept note-3: -Examples of personal fable can be seen in the following typified assertions made by adolescents: “Nobody understands me.” “My parents just don’t know what I’m going through–what do they know about what it’s like being a teenager?” “You just don’t know how it feels."
