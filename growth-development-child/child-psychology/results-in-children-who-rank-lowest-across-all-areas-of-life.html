EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -One study found that children of uninvolved parents perform poorly in social competence and psychosocial development. Children’s low self-esteem and confidence affect how they interact with other people. Lack of guidance from parents also can make children behave badly towards other people.

Concept note-2: -Uninvolved Parenting Children reared by parents who are low in both support and demandingness tend to rank lowest across all life domains, lack self-control, have low self-esteem, and are less competent than their peers.

Concept note-3: -Uninvolved parenting, sometimes referred to as neglectful parenting, is a style characterized by a lack of responsiveness to a child’s needs. Uninvolved parents make few to no demands of their children and they are often indifferent, dismissive, or even completely neglectful.

Concept note-4: -Examples of Uninvolved Parenting “Some [parents] may only provide basics like food and shelter for their kids, whereas others may have their children adhere to only basic rules, like wearing seat belts, but don’t pay attention to their kids’ physical or emotional needs on a larger scale, ” she describes.
