Moves the stage slightly to sharpen the image

(A) Body Tube

(B) Course Focus Knob

(C) Aperature

(D) ** Fine Focus Knob

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Focus (fine), Use the fine focus knob to sharpen the focus quality of the image after it has been brought into focus with the coarse focus knob.

Concept note-2: -The coarse adjustment knob quickly brings the image into focus, and the fine adjustment knobs help maintain focus as the magnification increases. Also Check: Compound Microscope. Types, Parts and Functions of a Microscope.

Concept note-3: -Compound microscopes have fine and coarse focus knobs which are used to – of course – achieve focus on the specimen. These knobs are also known as the ‘stage adjustment knobs’ because they literally adjust the stage up and down.
