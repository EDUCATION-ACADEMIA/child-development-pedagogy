____ and ____ are example of Hygiene Factors; while ____ and ____ are examples of Motivators.

(A) Company Policies and Growth; Achievement and Recognition

(B) ** Work Conditions and Salary; Achievement and Recognition

(C) Advancement and Work Conditions; Company Policies and Salary

(D) Salary and Recognition; Relationships and Supervision

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Examples of hygiene factors are work relationships, supervisor relationships, working conditions and environment, and salary. Examples of motivators are achievement, recognition, and responsibility.

Concept note-4: -The characteristics associated with job dissatisfaction are called hygiene factors.
