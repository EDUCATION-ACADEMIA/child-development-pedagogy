When adults are ready to learn, they

(A) come to class everyday

(B) actively participate in class

(C) ** are willing to learn what they need to know

(D) sit quietly in class

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Adults need to know why they need to learn something before undertaking to learn it. They need to know how learning will be conducted, what learning will occur and why learning is important.

Concept note-2: -Experiential learning makes the case that the essence of adult learning is making sense of experiences. Adults learn best when they learn by doing. Learning activities thus make heavy use of role-playing, simulations, and so on. Andragogy.

Concept note-3: -Adult learners appreciate working towards clearly-defined goals (this is their main motivator, and thatâ€™s why training must be short, relevant and useful). They hate the feeling of running around in circles and quickly become frustrated.
