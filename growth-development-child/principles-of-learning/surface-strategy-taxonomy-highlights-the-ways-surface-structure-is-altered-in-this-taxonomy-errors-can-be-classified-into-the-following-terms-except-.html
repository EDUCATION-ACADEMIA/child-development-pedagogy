Surface strategy taxonomy highlights the ways surface structure is altered; in this taxonomy, errors can be classified into the following terms EXCEPT ____

(A) omission

(B) ** morphology

(C) addition

(D) misformation.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Viewed from the perspective of surface strategy taxonomy, the errors are categorized into 5 groups; omission, addition, misformation, misardering, and blend.

Concept note-2: -Researchers have identified three broad types of error analysis according to the size of the sample. These types are: massive, specific and incidental samples.

Concept note-3: -Corder (1974) discusses the error analysis in five stages: collection of data containing errors, classification of errors, identification of errors, disclosure of errors and evaluation of errors.

Concept note-4: -Errors may also be classified according to the level of language: phonological errors, vocabulary or lexical errors, syntactic errors, and so on. They may be assessed according to the degree to which they interfere with communication: global errors make an utterance difficult to understand, while local errors do not.
