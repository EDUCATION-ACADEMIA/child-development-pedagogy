Which developmental indicator listed below best fit for the Emotional and Social Development domain?

(A) ** Show awareness of specific body parts.

(B) Transition from active to quiet activities with limited guidance and support.

(C) Speak clearly enough to be understood by most people.

(D) Use more complex and varied language to share ideas and influence others during play

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Physical development involves growth and changes in the body and brain, the senses, motor skills, and health and wellness. Cognitive development involves learning, attention, memory, language, thinking, reasoning, and creativity. Psychosocial development involves emotions, personality, and social relationships.

Concept note-2: -Physical, social, emotional and cognitive development.

Concept note-3: -Once a child has confidently developed movement (physical) they will be able to explore their environment, learning from their experiences (intellectual). This in turn will contribute to building confidence in expressing feelings and coping with different tasks (emotional).

Concept note-4: -Developmental milestones offer important clues about a childâ€™s developmental health. Reaching milestones at the typical ages shows a child is developing as expected. Reaching milestones much earlier means a child may be advanced compared with his or her peers of the same age.
