Blended Learning can best be described as any student completing work on a computer at home or outside of school.

(A) True

(B) ** False

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Blended Learning can best be described as any student completing work on a computer at home or outside of school. A student learns at least in part through online learning, with some element of control over time, place, and pace.

Concept note-2: -“Blended learning, also known as hybrid learning, is an approach to education that combines online educational materials and opportunities for interaction online with traditional place-based classroom methods.”

Concept note-3: -What is Blended Learning? Blended Learning is an education program that integrates a virtual and face-to-face learning environment for students.

Concept note-4: -Blended learning is an educational methodology that blends online or digital components with face-to-face instruction. Including technology in education helps set students up for success later in life, because computers and other connected devices are so integral to communication and business today.

Concept note-5: -Which of the following phrases best describes blended learning? It involves two teachers instructing a class together utilizing a team approach. It combines elements of face-to-face and online instruction.
