what is the economy doing? and how this affects sales

(A) Technological developments

(B) ** Economic issues

(C) Economic recession

(D) impact of external factors on product development

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -As the economy expands, businesses generally see an increase in sales or demand for their products. They will produce more goods and services to meet this increase in demand. As businesses need to produce more goods and services to meet demand, they need to hire more workers.

Concept note-2: -The United States economy has made an impressive recovery after COVID-19. The economy as a whole has returned to its pre-pandemic growth trajectory, with real GDP growing 2.1% in 2022, reports Statista.

Concept note-3: -The increase in the demand for goods/services within the economy means that firms are likely to experience an increase in sales revenue. This often causes an increase in the amount of profit that firms receive. Therefore, an increase in economic growth often benefits firms through increased revenues and profits.

Concept note-4: -Economic factors affect the economy, including interest rates, tax rates, laws, policies, wages, and governmental activities. These factors are not directly related to the business but influence the investment value in the future.
