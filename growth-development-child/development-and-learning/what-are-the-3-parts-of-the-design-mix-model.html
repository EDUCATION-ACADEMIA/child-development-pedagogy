What are the 3 parts of the design mix model?

(A) ** Function, Cost, Appearance

(B) Fact finding, Costing, looks

(C) Appearance, profit margin, testing

(D) research, design, production

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -These design factors-function, cost and aesthetics-are mixed together in different ways in order to appeal to different target markets . The design mix can be illustrated using a triangle to represent the three factors.

Concept note-2: -Cost. Companies need to ensure that products are economical to produce and to distribute. That way, the company can sell it profitably. Aesthetics. This factor is how a product can attract customers, especially, for example, in terms of appearance or taste.

Concept note-3: -Concrete Mix Design. Concrete design simply means choosing well-suited materials for good quality concrete and determining their relative quantities. The main purpose of designing concrete mix is to produce workable and durable concrete with the required strength.
