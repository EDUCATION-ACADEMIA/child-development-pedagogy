EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The difference with inTRApersonal intelligence is that it has more to do with an individual’s relationship to the world than the relationships between people. Someone with this intelligence would have a really good handle on his or her own views and opinions, feelings and anxieties, and even goals and dreams.

Concept note-2: -Bodily kinesthetic intelligence is the capacity to manipulate objects and use a variety of physical skills. This intelligence also involves a sense of timing and the perfection of skills through mind–body union. Athletes, dancers, surgeons, and crafts people exhibit well-developed bodily kinesthetic intelligence.

Concept note-3: -Bodily-Kinesthetic Intelligence According to the University of Tennessee, this type of intelligence relates to hand-eye coordination and motor skills. People with bodily-kinesthetic intelligence excel at physical movement, which makes them skilled at dancing and sports.

Concept note-4: -People with a strong intrapersonal intelligence have a deep awareness of their feelings, ideas, needs, and goals. Students with this intelligence usually need time alone to process and create.
