CHILD DEVELOPMENT AND PEDAGOGY

[SOURCES]
a form of small-group learning in which students work together to achieve a common goal

(A) ** cooperative learning

(B) group work

(C) team work

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In formal cooperative learning students work together for one or more class periods to complete a joint task or assignment (Johnson et al., 2014). There are several features that can help these groups work well: The instructor defines the learning objectives for the activity and assigns students to groups.

Concept note-2: -0 SMALL-GROUP cooperative learning is a classroom learning en-vironment where students work together in small heterogeneous. groups on academic tasks. Within such groups students are encouraged. to share ideas and help each other learn.

Concept note-3: -Cooperative Learning is an instructional method in which students work in small groups to accomplish a common learning goal with the guidance of the teacher.

Concept note-4: -Small group learning is an educational approach that focuses on individuals learning in small groups and is distinguished from learning climate and organizational learning. It is also described as a team-based approach to learning where students work together towards shared learning objectives.

Concept note-5: -What is Cooperative Learning? Cooperative Learning involves structuring classes around small groups that work together in such a way that each group member’s success is dependent on the group’s success.
