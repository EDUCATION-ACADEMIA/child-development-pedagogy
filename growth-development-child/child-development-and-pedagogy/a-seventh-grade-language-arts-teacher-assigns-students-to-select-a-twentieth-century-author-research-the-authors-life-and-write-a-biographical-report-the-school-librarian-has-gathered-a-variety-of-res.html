Concept note-1: -Classroom-based action research (CBAR) typically involves teachers conducting collaborative, evidence-based investigations into their own classroom routines and relationships with a view to understanding and improving the quality and justice of their practices in the classroom.

Concept note-2: -Action Research is a method of systematic enquiry that teachers undertake as researchers of their own practice.

Concept note-3: -In a research-based learning approach, students actively search for and then use multiple resources, materials, and texts in order to explore important, relevant, and interesting questions and challenges.
