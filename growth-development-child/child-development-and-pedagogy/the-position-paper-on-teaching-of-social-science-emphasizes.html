The position paper on Teaching of Social Science emphasizes

(A) ** Respecting different opinions and examining ideas and practices.

(B) Accept social hierarchies.

(C) Increasing the use of textbooks.

(D) Encouraging memorization.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -NCF 2005 has published a position paper on the teaching of Social Science which emphasizes respecting different opinions and examining ideas and practices as Social Science is a subject which emphasizes upon: enhancing visions and perspectives about society. studying human behaviour and making socially bonded person.

Concept note-2: -The teaching of social sciences must adopt methods that promote creativity, aesthetics, and critical perspectives, and enable children to draw relationships between past and present, to understand changes taking place in society.

Concept note-3: -Study of Social Science makes us an efficient citizen of a democracy, and it also helps us to solve the practical problems in our daily life. It is essential for communities and organizations. It also helps the students to know how different societies are managed, structured and governed.

Concept note-4: -The goal of any social studies teacher is to help students learn from the past so they can become better informed, and help themselves and their societies in the future.

Concept note-5: -Thus, social sciences help people understand how to interact with the social world-how to influence policy, develop networks, increase government accountability, and promote democracy.
