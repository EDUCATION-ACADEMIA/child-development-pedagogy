What is most important for a school to help social change?

(A) Discipline

(B) Traditionalism

(C) Nationalism

(D) ** Modernization

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The socialization of the young generation and maintenance of proper social order is among one of the main functions of education. It not only acts as a mean to bring about social changes in a society, but also to excel the rate of such changes.

Concept note-2: -Education can initiate social changes by bringing about a change in outlook and attitude of man. It can bring about a change in the pattern of social relationships and thereby it may cause social changes. Education can be used as a tool to empower the individual.

Concept note-3: -Education is the backbone of any society and it plays a vital role in the process of modernization. It is through education that people learn about new ideas, technologies and ways of living. Education helps to spread knowledge and understanding about different cultures and customs.

Concept note-4: -It is a movement from traditional to modern where certain things changes for better life. It helps to nation for establishing its own identity also helps to meet the needs of people to discover new technologies and rapid growth in modernization scientifically. It also enriches the economic life of people.

Concept note-5: -The functions of education in the sphere of social change are outlines as under: Assistance in changing attitudes. Education helps to change the attitudes of people in favor of modern ways of life and develops attitudes, which can fight prejudice, superstitions and traditional beliefs.
