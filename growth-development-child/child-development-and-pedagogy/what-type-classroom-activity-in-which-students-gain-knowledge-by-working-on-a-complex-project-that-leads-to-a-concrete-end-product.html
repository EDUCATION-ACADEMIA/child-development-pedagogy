What type classroom activity in which students gain knowledge by working on a complex project that leads to a concrete end product?

(A) Blended Learning

(B) ** Project Based Learning

(C) Informal Learning

(D) Flipped Classroom

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Project-based learning (PBL) or project-based instruction is an instructional approach designed to give students the opportunity to develop knowledge and skills through engaging projects set around challenges and problems they may face in the real world.

Concept note-2: -Hence, it could be concluded that the constructive type of projects helps in the knowledge building of the students.

Concept note-3: -They demonstrate their knowledge and skills by creating a public product or presentation for a real audience. As a result, students develop deep content knowledge as well as critical thinking, collaboration, creativity, and communication skills.

Concept note-4: -Project Based Learning (PBL) is a model and framework of teaching and learning where students acquire content knowledge and skills in order to answer a driving question based on an authentic problem, need, challenge, or concern.
