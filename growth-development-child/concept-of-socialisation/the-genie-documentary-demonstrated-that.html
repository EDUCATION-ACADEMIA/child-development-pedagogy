CONCEPT OF SOCIALISATION

[SOURCES]
The “Genie” documentary demonstrated that

(A) ** long-term social isolation leads to permanent damage

(B) short-term social isolation leads to permanent damage

(C) long-term social isolation does not lead to permanent damage

(D) short-term social isolation does not lead to permanent damage

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The story of Genie shows the importance of socialization in human society. Socialization refers to preparing newcomers to become members of an existing group and to think, feel, and act in ways the group considers appropriate. Viewed from the group’s point of view, it is a process of member replacement.

Concept note-2: -At the age of 13, Genie possessed inhuman characteristics including a strange body walk and was not able to talk or make sounds. Genie was still wearing diapers at her age and often sniffled and clawed characteristics that are not manifested under “normal” socialization process.

Concept note-3: -Socialization is a process of internalizing the norms, culture, values and customs of the society to be socially acceptable.

Concept note-4: -What are three modes of socialization? Socialization occurs through explicit instruction, conditioning and innovation and role modeling. In practice, these modes are usually blended.
