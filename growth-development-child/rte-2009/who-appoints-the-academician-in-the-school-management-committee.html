RTE 2009

Who appoints the academician in the school management committee?

(A) Chairman

(B) Elected Panchayat Member Shri

(C) Member Secretary

(D) ** Guardian members of the child

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -They give importance to giving boys and girls equal opportunity and stimulating learning environment. Teachers are realizing the benefit of giving space to Parents through SMC and in return being able to enjoy the improvement in the functioning of their school.

Concept note-2: -The SMCs consist of representatives of the local authority, parents or guardians of students, and the principal and teachers of the school. Three-fourths of SMC members must be parents or guardians and half must be women.

Concept note-3: -Functions of School Management Committee (i) (ii) (iii) Monitor the working of the school. Prepare and recommend School Development Plan. Monitor the utilization of the grants received from the appropriate Government or Local Authority or any other source.

Concept note-4: -In fact, 75% of the SMC members should be parents. 1/3 should be from the elected members of the local authority, 1/3 from amongst the teachers and 1/3 from amongst the local educationists. In case, there is no educationist in the area, 1 student should take the place.
