How many days of teaching work in class 1 to 5 per year?

(A) ** 200 days

(B) 180 days

(C) 220 days

(D) 240 days

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Working Days in an academic year : Class 1 to 5 – 200 days. Class 6 to 8 – 220 days.

Concept note-2: -Mandatory 220 Working Days in an Academic Year in the School. Section 19 of RTE Act-2009 envisages that every recognized school has to observe the minimum of 220 Working Days for Upper Primary Classes in an Academic Year.

Concept note-3: -According to the guidelines laid down by CBSE, every teacher is supposed to be devoting as many as 1, 200 hours a year to teaching in class, out of which 200 should be kept for remedial or substitution classes.

Concept note-4: -Hence, from the given points, it is clear that the working hours per week for teachers are 45 hours. Rajasthan 3rd Grade Teacher Recruitment for Level 1 & Level 2 will be done through the scores of REET 2022.
