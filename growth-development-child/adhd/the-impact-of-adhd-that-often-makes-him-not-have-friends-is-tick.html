ADHD

[SOURCES]
the impact of ADHD that often makes him not have friends is (tick)

(A) ** cut off the conversation

(B) ** Can’t wait to play

(C) ** borrowed pencils and not permission

(D) keep friends’ belongings in their place

(E) neatly set the table

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -If you have ADHD, you might find it hard to date, make friends, or parent. That’s partly because good relationships require you to be aware of other people’s thoughts and feelings. But ADHD can make it hard for you to pay attention or react the right way.

Concept note-2: -Adults with ADHD may find it difficult to focus and prioritize, leading to missed deadlines and forgotten meetings or social plans. The inability to control impulses can range from impatience waiting in line or driving in traffic to mood swings and outbursts of anger.

Concept note-3: -Whether you collect new friends easily or lean on a few, long-term friendships dating back to kindergarten, there’s no wrong way to build relationships. This is true especially for people with ADHD, who often report that their symptoms complicate, challenge, and color friendships.
