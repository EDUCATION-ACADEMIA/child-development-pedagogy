Concept note-1: -Typesy can also be used for district-level typing tests. Rapid Typing Tutor and Typing.com are available free for those who do not want to spend a lot. The Typing Cat is recommended for those who work in a specific field and want a field-based typing learning experience.

Concept note-2: -Ans 1. There are three stages involved in touch typing technique: Stage one of the process begins with learning the home row of the keyboard (the row beginning with the Caps Lock key). Looking at the keyboard is strictly forbidden.

Concept note-3: -Touch typing is typing without looking at the keyboard. The fundamental idea is that each finger is given its own section of the keyboard and your fingers learn the location of the keyboard through practicing regularly and gaining muscle memory to eventually build up speed whilst typing.
