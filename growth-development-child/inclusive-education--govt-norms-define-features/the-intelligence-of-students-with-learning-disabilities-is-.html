The intelligence of students with learning disabilities is ____

(A) ** of average intelligence

(B) of lower than average intelligence

(C) of average or above average intelligence

(D) all the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Students with specific learning disabilities have average to above average intelligence but may have difficulties acquiring and/or demonstrating knowledge and understanding content.

Concept note-2: -Learning disabled children are mostly underachievers. A person with an IQ of 20-34, a severe learning disability. A person with an IQ of 35-49, moderate learning disability. A person with an IQ of 50-70, mild learning disability.

Concept note-3: -Their IQ score ranges from 50-70, and they can often acquire academic skills up to about the sixth-grade level. They can become fairly self-sufficient and in some cases live independently, with community and social support.

Concept note-5: -People with Moderate Learning Difficulties have an IQ of between 35-50. They generally reach developmental milestones much more slowly than their peers do, and learn at a slower pace.
