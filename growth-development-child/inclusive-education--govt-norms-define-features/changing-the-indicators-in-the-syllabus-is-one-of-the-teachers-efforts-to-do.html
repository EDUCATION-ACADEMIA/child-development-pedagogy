Changing the indicators in the syllabus is one of the teacher’s efforts to do?

(A) ** Curriculum Modification

(B) Curriculum Adaptation

(C) Both are right

(D) Making PPIs

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A curriculum modification is a change to a classroom activity or to the classroom materials in order to maximize a child’s participation. These changes aren’t big, but they are intentional.

Concept note-2: -Seven key indicators are discussed in this paper: A clear mission, vision and goals; effective learning environment; availability of physical resources and facilities; adequacy of teachers; continued professional development opportunities; teacher and student motivation and effective supervision and leadership.

Concept note-3: -They require information/knowledge both about the background to the new curriculum (which would include information about the approach and the design) and about how they will be expected to manage it, taking on responsibilities for example for designing materials themselves that they may not have had before.

Concept note-4: -There are various factors that influence Curriculum Implementation like the learners, resource materials and facilities, the teacher, the school environment, culture and ideology, instructional supervision and assessment.
