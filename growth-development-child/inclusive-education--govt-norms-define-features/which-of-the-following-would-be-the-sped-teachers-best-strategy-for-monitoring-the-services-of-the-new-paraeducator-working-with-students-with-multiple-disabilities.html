(D) ** Enlisting the help of a gen ed teacher in planning and supervising the work of paraeducator teachers

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -7. Which of the following approaches is best for a teacher to take to increase students’ awareness about local cultures? Concept note-2: -Establishing a positive learning environment where the focus is on both learning and positives will go a long way in curbing student behaviors. If they know, as students, that you will meet their needs and remain positive, they will be less likely to show you those negative behaviors.
