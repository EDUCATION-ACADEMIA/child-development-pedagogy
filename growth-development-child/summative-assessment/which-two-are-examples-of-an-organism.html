Which TWO are examples of an organism?

(A) ** Dog

(B) Chair

(C) Plastic toy

(D) ** Plant

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Organisms are characterized into two broad categories based upon how they obtain their energy and nutrients: autotrophs and heterotrophs. Autotrophs are known as producers because they are able to make their own food from raw materials and energy. Examples include plants, algae, and some types of bacteria.

Concept note-2: -Plants are one of the two major groups of living organisms that are an essential entity to the function of the biosphere. Plants can be found in all known parts of the earth, in all shapes and sizes. They include the green algae, mosses, ferns, vines, grasses, bushes, herbs, flowering plants and trees.

Concept note-3: -An organism generally refers to a single or individual living species, which exhibits all the properties of life. These can be a plant, animal, bird, insect and even a microbe.

Concept note-4: -organism: an individual form of life, such as a plant, animal or bacterium. photosynthesis: process by which green plants use sunlight to make food using carbon dioxide and water.
