What were the original two elements in our universe?

(A) Lithium and Carbon

(B) Hydrogen and Neon

(C) Helium and Chlorine

(D) ** Hydrogen and Helium

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In the beginning, or at least following the Big Bang more than 14 billion years ago, there was hydrogen, some helium and a little bit of lithium.
