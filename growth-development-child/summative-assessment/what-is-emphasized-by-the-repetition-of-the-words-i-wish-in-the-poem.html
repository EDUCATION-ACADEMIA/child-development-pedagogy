What is emphasized by the repetition of the words “ I wish” in the poem?

(A) The reason the speaker wants her mother to like Manga

(B) ** The speaker’s desire for Manga to have the best life possible

(C) The length of time it takes the speaker to find Manga when he escapes

(D) The speaker’s reason for keeping Manga as a pet

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Alliteration: repetition of initial or medial consonants in two or more adjacent words. Repeated consonant sounds at the beginning of words is called initial alliteration. Repeated consonant sounds in the middle or at the ends of words is called internal alliteration. Repetition of vowel sounds is called assonance.

Concept note-2: -How does the repetition of “if” most contribute to the poem’s overall meaning? It highlights how fearful the speaker is about his son’s future. It highlights how unlikely it is that the speaker’s son will become a proper adult.

Concept note-3: -Lenore is the speaker’s friend whom the speaker is currently in love with.

Concept note-4: -The speaker wants his daughter to go to school and get a degree.
