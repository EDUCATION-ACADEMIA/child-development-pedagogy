SUMMATIVE ASSESSMENT

[SOURCES]
I would take him for a walk everyday if I ____

(A) have a pet dog.

(B) ** had a pet dog.

(C) will have a pet dog.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Walking: Walking should be part of every dog’s daily routine to keep them physically and mentally healthy. Most dogs need at least 1-2 walks per day (unless otherwise specified by your vet).

Concept note-2: -They’re both grammatically correct, and equally comprehensible. The only distinction I would make is that ‘I took my dog for a walk’ sounds slightly more informal and relaxed, so I think most people would tend to say this rather than ‘i walked my dog’. I walked the dog.

Concept note-3: -One of the foremost reasons why you need to take your dog out is to so that they can actively play and mix well with other dogs in the park or neighbourhood. This hones their social skills for the further run.

Concept note-4: -Dogs need regular exercise, just like people. If your dog isn’t getting enough physical activity, he may put on too much weight, become bored or frustrated, or release his pent-up energy in undesirable ways. Keep in mind that individual dogs have different needs.
