Fifteen days ____ quite long for Christmas vacation.

(A) ** is

(B) are

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Christmas vacation in the United States is generally two weeks for students. This includes elementary and high school students. What is this? While the dates can move around, Christmas vacation will encompass both Christmas and New Year’s Day.

Concept note-2: -"It’s a beaut, Clark, it’s a beaut!”-Clark Griswold Sr.

Concept note-3: -25 December will be a public holiday in most countries around the world. If 25 December falls on a weekend, then a nearby week day may be taken as a holiday in lieu.

Concept note-4: -A week of autumn break is usually scheduled during the week of All Saints’ Day (November 1). The winter-or Christmas holiday lasts two weeks and encompasses both Christmas and New Year’s Day. The dates for both the one-week spring break and the two-week Easter holiday vary.
