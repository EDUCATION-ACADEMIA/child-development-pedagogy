Which of the following is a homogeneous mixture?

(A) beef stew

(B) mud

(C) ** salt water

(D) rocky road ice cream

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Whereas salt dissolves in water and the composition of salt in water is same at any point in the mixture, so it is a homogenous mixture.

Concept note-2: -Saltwater acts as if it were a single substance even though it contains two substances-salt and water. Saltwater is a homogeneous mixture, or a solution.

Concept note-3: -Homogeneous mixtures â€“ Soda water, air, vinegar, alcohol and water mixture, sugar and water mixture, Copper sulphate solution.

Concept note-4: -A homogeneous mixture is one in which the constituents are uniformly distributed throughout the volume and cannot be seen separately. For example, salt solution. Therefore, a salt solution is homogeneous.

Concept note-5: -A homogeneous mixture is a mixture in which the composition is uniform throughout the mixture. The salt water described above is homogeneous because the dissolved salt is evenly distributed throughout the entire salt water sample.
