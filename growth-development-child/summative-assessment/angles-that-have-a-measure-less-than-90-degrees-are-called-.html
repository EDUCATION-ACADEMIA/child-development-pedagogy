Angles that have a measure less than 90 degrees are called ____

(A) ** acute angles

(B) obtuse angles

(C) right angles

(D) straight angles

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Angles between 0 and 90 degrees (0°< <90°) are called acute angles. Angles between 90 and 180 degrees (90°< <180°) are known as obtuse angles. Angles that are 90 degrees ( = 90°) are right angles. Angles that are 180 degrees ( = 180°) are known as straight angles.

Concept note-2: -Acute angles measure less than 90 degrees. Right angles measure 90 degrees. Obtuse angles measure more than 90 degrees.

Concept note-3: -An angle which measures less than 90° is called an acute angle. The measure between 0° to 90°.
