The ratio 1:4 is equivalent to:

(A) ** 4:16

(B) 6:12

(C) 5/15

(D) 2/9

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Since the simplest form of the fraction 4/16 is 1/4, the simplest form of the ratio 4:16 is also 1:4.

Concept note-2: -Answer: The fractions equivalent to 1/4 are 2/8, 3/12, 4/16, etc. Equivalent fractions have the same value in their reduced form. Explanation: Equivalent fractions can be written by multiplying or dividing both the numerator and the denominator by the same number.

Concept note-3: -Equivalent fractions of 1/4: 2/8, 3/12, 4/16, 5/20.

Concept note-4: -There are many fractions that are equal to 1/4. Some of these fractions are 2/8, 4/16, 8/32, and 10/40. These fractions all have the same value, but they have different numerators and denominators. The numerator is the top number in a fraction, and the denominator is the bottom number.

Concept note-5: -Answer: 16 to the power of 1/4 is 2. Let us see the solution step by step.
