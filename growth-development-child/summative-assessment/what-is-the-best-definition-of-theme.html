What is the best definition of THEME?

(A) ** A universal truth or message that the author wants to express.

(B) The location and time of the story

(C) The events that occur in the story

(D) The author’s reason for writing a story

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The term theme can be defined as the underlying meaning of a story. It is the message the writer is trying to convey through the story. Often the theme of a story is a broad message about life. The theme of a story is important because a story’s theme is part of the reason why the author wrote the story.

Concept note-2: -(iːm ) noun. an idea or topic expanded in a discourse, discussion, etc. (in literature, music, art, etc) a unifying idea, image, or motif, repeated or developed throughout a work.

Concept note-3: -A universal theme is an idea that applies to anyone regardless of cultural differences, or geographic location. Universal themes are ways to connect ideas across all disciplines. It is a central idea about the human condition.

Concept note-4: -What is the best definition of THEME? A universal truth, moral, or idea that the author wants to express.

Concept note-5: -A theme is a central, unifying idea. It’s the bigger issue that emerges as the characters pursue their goals.
