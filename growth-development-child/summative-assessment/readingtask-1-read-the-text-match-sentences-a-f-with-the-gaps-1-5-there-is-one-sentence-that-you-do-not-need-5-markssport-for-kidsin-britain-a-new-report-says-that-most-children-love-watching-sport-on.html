Concept note-1: -10 Lines On The Importance Of Sports Everyone loves to play games. It plays an important part in improving physical and mental health. A good sport helps in building confidence and leadership skills. There are two types of gaming activities – indoor and outdoor.

Concept note-2: -Treat the other team with respect and never tease or bully. Follow the rules of the game. Help another player up who has fallen. Take pride in winning but don’t rub it in.

Concept note-3: -Have a positive attitude. Give your best effort. Shake hands with the other team before and after the game. Support teammates by saying “good shot” or “good try.” Never criticize a teammate for trying.
