Concept note-1: -Which of the following is true about sleep disturbances in early childhood? Most sleep disturbances persist into adulthood and require medical help for their resolution.

Concept note-2: -3-4 years: preschooler development. At 3-4 years, there might be emotional developments, early friendships, longer sentences, a stronger memory, new physical skills and more. Concept note-3: -Sleep plays a critical role in development in children; it allows for their bodies to recharge and retain the information they have learned throughout the day. In toddlers, napping allows for memory consolidation, motor skill development, and executive attention.

Concept note-4: -18 Month Sleep Regression FAQ A: The most common causes of night waking at this age are overtiredness and sleep onset associations. Children who skip naps or go to bed too late may be overtired, resulting in more wakings.
