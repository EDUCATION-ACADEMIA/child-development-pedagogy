The ____ is the carrier of the message of the means by which the message sent.

(A) encoder

(B) decoder

(C) noise

(D) ** channel

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Answer. Explanation: A communication channel is the medium, mean, manner or method through which a message is sent to its intended receiver.

Concept note-2: -The medium through which a communication message travels, is known as channel. The sender sends the message which needs to get delivered through a path which is known as communication channel. Disturbance in the communication channel hampers the proper decoding of the message by the receiver.

Concept note-3: -The sender is the person who transmits the message. The person who transmits a message to the other person is called the sender, and the person who receives the message is called the receiver.

Concept note-4: -Also called the channel, the medium is the means by which a message is transmitted.

Concept note-5: -Sender: This is the person that is delivering a message to a recipient. Message: This refers to the information that the sender is relaying to the receiver. Channel of communication: This is the transmission or method of delivering the message.
