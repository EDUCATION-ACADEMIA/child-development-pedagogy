Which illustrates the desire for comfort from parents of a typical four-year-old?

(A) Larry asked his mother to come see the puzzle he put together.

(B) Matt knew how to control his temper and never argued.

(C) Norma played by herself most of the time.

(D) ** Olivia wanted her mom to sit with her when she felt sick.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -which illustrates the desire for comfort from parents of a typical four year old? Olivia wanted her mom to sit with her when she felt badly.

Concept note-2: -How are the cognitive characteristics of a three-year-old different from a four-year-old? Four-year-olds figure out solutions to difficult situations more easily than three-year-olds. Four-year-olds understand instructions when given seven things to do; three-year-olds, when given four things to do.

Concept note-3: -In the preoperational stage, children use symbols to represent words, images, and ideas, which is why children in this stage engage in pretend play. Concept note-4: -Which characteristics illustrate the development of six-year-olds? Concentrates on an activity for long time periods; wants to be right and to win. Generates ideas and suggestions; follows two-step directions. May have a best friend; can follow three commands in sequence.
