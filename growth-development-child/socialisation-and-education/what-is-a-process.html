What is a process?

(A) A complex phenomenon

(B) ** A sequence of steps

(C) A sudden event

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A sequence of steps is when a process is written in the order that tasks were done. When a sequential pattern of organization is used, the information is arranged according to a step-by-step sequence that describes a specific process.

Concept note-2: -A sequence is a set of numbers in a particular order or a set of numbers that follow a pattern. The most basic sequential order example is that of counting numbers 1, 2, 3, 4 and so on. The numbers follow an increasing pattern (1, 2, 3â€¦).

Concept note-3: -Why is sequencing important? We sequence all day long-we divide our time into what we need to do first, second, and last; we understand events in our lives by understanding the order in which they occur. For some children, sequencing can be a hard concept to grasp, especially when they are trying to tell a story.
