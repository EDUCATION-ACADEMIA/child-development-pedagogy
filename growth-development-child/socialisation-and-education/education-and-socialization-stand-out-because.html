SOCIALISATION AND EDUCATION

[SOURCES]
Education and socialization stand out because:

(A) Socialization is conscious and education rather unconscious

(B) ** Socialization is rather unconscious and conscious education

(C) It’s the same thing

(D) It is not the same people who educate and socialize the children

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -EDUCATION AS A PROCESS OF SOCIALIZATION The school teaches the accepted ways of behaviour within the group to the child. The child learn the patterns of behaviour needed to good life in the society . They learn their roles and roles of the other in the school and thus socialization takes place in the school.

Concept note-2: -Freud’s Theory of Socialization According to Sigmund Freud, “basic biological instincts combine with societal factors to shape personalities.” Freud emphasizes that a major part of human personality is formed in the childhood and during rest of the life it is elaborated and sharpened.
