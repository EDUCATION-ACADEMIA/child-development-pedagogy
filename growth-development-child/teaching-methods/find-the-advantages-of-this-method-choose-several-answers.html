TEACHING METHODS

[SOURCES]
Find the advantages of this method. (Choose several answers).

(A) ** Training in self-expression

(B) Emphasis on Teaching instead of Learning

(C) ** Training in reflective thinking

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -As stated in an online article in Educause Quarterly, “Reflective learning can aid learners in synthesizing new information, and it is often used to improve reading comprehension, writing performance, and self-esteem via self-examination."

Concept note-2: -Reflection gives the brain an opportunity to pause amidst the chaos, untangle and sort through observations and experiences, consider multiple possible interpretations, and create meaning. This meaning becomes learning, which can then inform future mindsets and actions.

Concept note-3: -Reflective thinking means taking the bigger picture and understanding all of its consequences. It doesn’t mean that you’re just going to simply write down your future plans or what you’ve done in the past. It means truly trying to understand why you did what you did, and why that’s important.

Concept note-4: -Reflection is a cyclical process: experience, analysis, implement, repeat.
