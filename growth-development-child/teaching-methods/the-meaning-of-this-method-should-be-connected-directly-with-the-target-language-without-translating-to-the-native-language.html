GROWTH DEVELOPMENT CHILD

TEACHING METHODS

[SOURCES]
The meaning of this method should be connected directly with the target language without translating to the native language.

(A) Indirect Method

(B) ** Direct Method

(C) Community Language Learning Method

(D) Total Physical Response Method

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Direct method: It is also known as the natural and anti-grammatical method, it facilitates the active participation of students. It focuses on speaking skills and emphasizes to learn the target language directly without translating it into the mother tongue.

Concept note-2: -The Direct Method is based on the direct involvement of the student when speaking, and listening to, the foreign language in common everyday situations. Consequently, there is lots of oral interaction, spontaneous use of the language, no translation, and little if any analysis of grammar rules and syntax.

Concept note-3: -The direct method of teaching, which is sometimes called the natural method, and is often (but not exclusively) used in teaching foreign languages, refrains from using the learnersâ€™ native language and uses only the target language.

Concept note-4: -The Direct Method The process consists of the gradual acquisition of grammatical structure and vocabulary. The learner is encouraged to think in the target language rather than translate.

Concept note-5: -Grammar-Translation In this method, learning is largely by translation to and from the target language. Grammar rules are to be memorized and long lists of vocabulary learned by heart. There is little or no emphasis placed on developing oral ability. This method is most commonly used in secondary education.
