Which is not true about projects

(A) It is a purposeful activity

(B) It is accomplished in real life

(C) ** It is teacher centred activity

(D) It is proceeded in social environment

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is option (D) It is teacher-centered activity. Explanation: The statement which is not true about the project is that It is teacher-centered activity. A project is purposeful activity which is proceeded in social environment and is also accomplished in reallife.

Concept note-2: -Kilpatrick devised four classes of projects for his method: construction (such as writing a play), enjoyment (such as experiencing a concert), problem (for instance, discussing a complex social problem like poverty), and specific learning (learning of skills such as swimming).

Concept note-3: -It is a problem-solving activity and provides an opportunity for developing intellectually as well as doing abilities.

Concept note-4: -The teacher has an important part to play in the initiation, implementation, and culmination of projects. Initially, the teacher must determine the appropriateness of the topic to be investigated. Projects are productive and enlightening if the topic under investigation is interesting to most of the children.
